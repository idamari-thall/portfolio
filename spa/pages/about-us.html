About Us

Bed of Roses

We opened our wellness resort 15 years ago, with the intention of combining the calmness of the Swedish countryside to the experience of massage therapy, as well as body and mind relaxation practices. We take extreme care of our guests and we ensure calmness and peace for those who decide to join us at the SPA.

Our location

Situated in the lovely fields of Skåne, Bed of Roses is perfect for you who are looking for an escape from the city. Our spa is both ideal for couples, friend groups and even solo visitors. Enjoy the beautiful view and the peacefullness of nature with us, just outside Helsingborg.
